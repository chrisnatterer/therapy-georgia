## Individual Therapy

I offer humanistic and existential therapy for individuals. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, life transitions, relationship difficulties, or existential questions, therapy provides a space to explore what matters most to you.

### Single Session — $50

A 50-minute individual therapy session, available online or in-person in Tbilisi.

Ideal if you:
- Want to try therapy without a long-term commitment
- Need support with a specific situation
- Are looking for a one-time consultation

### 3-Session Package — $125 (Save $25)

Three 50-minute sessions for those ready to go deeper. Continuity allows us to build on each session and explore patterns that a single meeting can't reach.

Ideal if you:
- Want to commit to a short-term therapeutic process
- Are working through a life transition or ongoing challenge
- Prefer the accountability of scheduled sessions

## How It Works

1. **Reach out** — Send me a message via WhatsApp or book directly through Cal.com
2. **First session** — We'll talk about what brings you to therapy and what you're hoping for
3. **Ongoing work** — If you choose to continue, we'll establish a rhythm that works for you

## What to Expect

- **Confidential** — Everything discussed in session stays between us
- **Non-judgmental** — There's no right or wrong way to do therapy
- **Your pace** — We go at the speed that feels right for you
- **Flexible** — Sessions available in English or Georgian, online or in-person
